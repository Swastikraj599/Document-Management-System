•	This is the Document Management System. 
•	Here, you can store your important documents and can share them with others. 
•	This tool is very useful for the corporates for the management of their important documents. 
•	It is a secure application where no third party person can access the documents without prior permissions. 
•	You can create permissions and assign them what they can do with the documents, whether it is editing, updating or else things.
